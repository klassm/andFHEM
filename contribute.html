Can I support you?

App development is very time intensive. I am happy if you think the app is great and want to support the future development. There's a premium version available, which currently only removes some few advertisements within the app. It does not have any more features, as I do not want to force anyone to buy the version. However, feel free to do so!

If you think this symbolical amount of money for the premium version is too less and want to donate some more money, feel free to use Paypal.
